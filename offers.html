How Bonuses Work

A wagering requirement is the number of times you need to play through a bonus amount before you can withdraw any winnings. For example, if you have a $10 bonus with a 20x wagering requirement, you need to bet $200 before withdrawing.

Most bonuses can be activated by clicking the "Activate Now" button, or by making a qualifying deposit. Some may require a bonus code which you enter during the deposit process.

No, bonus money usually cannot be withdrawn immediately. It must first meet the specified wagering requirements. Once the requirements are met, the bonus funds and any associated winnings typically convert to real money which can then be withdrawn.
